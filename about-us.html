About Campus‑Vibe

Your one‑stop platform to explore, participate, and relive the best moments of campus life!

Who We Are

Campus‑Vibe is a modern event‑management platform designed exclusively for college students to stay connected with every happening on campus. Our mission is to make event discovery simple, participation seamless, and memories unforgettable.

What We Do

From cultural fests and tech events to seminars, workshops, and sports activities — we bring all campus events into one unified place. Students can browse upcoming events, register instantly, view event highlights, and explore photo galleries of past celebrations.

Our Vision

We aim to transform how students engage with campus life by combining simplicity, creativity, and technology. Our goal is to ensure that no student ever misses an opportunity to participate, learn, or enjoy college experiences.

Our Team

Campus-Vibe is built by a passionate group of final-year students who believe in enhancing student engagement using technology. Our team consists of skilled frontend developers, backend developers, UI/UX designers, and concept creators working together to bring this project to life.
